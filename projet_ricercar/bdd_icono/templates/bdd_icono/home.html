The ICONO Ricercar site provides a service designed to enhance the longevity and efficiency of the RICERCAR laboratory's iconographic catalog. Our platform offers researchers an effective and user-friendly tool, including a lexicon tailored to musical iconography.

Led by a student team from the Centre d’études supérieures de la Renaissance (CESR), the project is part of an initiative to modernize and integrate resources, in line with contemporary needs of researchers and cultural institutions. The stress on interoperability ensures smooth and sustainable data integration, making it an asset for those working in the field.

Users can view image previews directly on the site, although high-resolution downloads require authorization. The database features a rich and varied collection of iconographic materials, spanning from the Middle Ages to the Renaissance, inviting exploration and discovery
